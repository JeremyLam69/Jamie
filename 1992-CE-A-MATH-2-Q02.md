## 2(a)

$(1+3x)^2(1+x)^n$
$\displaystyle = (1+6x+9x^2)(1+nx+{{n(n-1)} \over 2}x^2 + \cdots)$
$\displaystyle = 1+nx+{{n(n-1)} \over 2}x^2 + 6x(1+nx) + 9x^2(1)  + \cdots$
$\displaystyle = 1 + (n+6)x + [{{n(n-1)} \over 2} + 6n + 9]x^2 + \cdots$

Coefficient of x is 10
$\Rightarrow n+6=10$
$\Rightarrow n=4$
